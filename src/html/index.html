Our Process

Empathize

Understand the pain points of the problem you are aiming to solve, the thought process and emotions of your audience at every touchpoint, and current and future market landscapes.



Define

Abstracting the problem to its highest level allows you to see the entire problem at a macrolevel. The results confirm if the original problem is the actual problem to be solved or a byproduct of a larger problem. We then break this category down into two components: 1.) What are we trying to accomplish for the business? and 2.) What are we trying to accomplish for the customer/user?. The end result is a “defined problem to be solved” which address both questions.



Ideate

Leverage Design Thinking methodologies to develop innovative strategies to craft solutions to the “problem to be solved”. We use Divergent Thinking and Convergent Thinking to generate ideas, then compare and contrast strategies before finalizing a go to market strategy.



Prototype & Test

Develop, test, and improve ideas with key stakeholders and users before full-scale implementation occurs. Our “Test” workflow creates human centric experiences for unbiased feedback that delivers insightful and actionable data.
